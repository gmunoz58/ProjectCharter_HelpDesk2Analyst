Project Overview

Project Title & Description

Title

Help Desk to Analyst: Strategic Career Transition Project

Project Overview

This project focuses on developing a structured plan to transition from a Help Desk role into a Business/Data Analyst position. It will deliver a refined professional presence, targeted networking efforts, insight gathered from industry professionals, and tailored application materials. The result will be a clear and actionable pathway supporting my move into an analyst role.

Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment

Personal Mission Connection

My personal mission is to help bridge the gap between business and technology by creating clarity, offering guidance, and improving systems. Moving into an analyst role expands this mission by allowing me to use data-driven insights to support decisions and improve organizational outcomes. This project directly advances the type of professional I strive to become.

Vision Alignment

My long-term vision is to build a career centered on analytical thinking, problem-solving, and strategic contribution. Transitioning from Help Desk to an analyst role aligns with that vision by positioning me in work that reflects my strengths and future goals. This project provides the structure and direction needed to make that shift intentional and achievable.

Strategy Goal Support

This project supports my strategic goals by strengthening my professional brand, expanding my network, and preparing targeted application materials. Each deliverable contributes directly to the skills and visibility required for analyst roles. Together, these actions advance my overall career trajectory in a focused and practical way.

Business Case & Justification

Business Case & Justification

Professional Value

Professionally, this project will enhance my competitiveness for Business/Data Analyst positions by improving my LinkedIn presence, strengthening my network, and aligning my experience with industry expectations. It directly supports my transition into a more analytical, growth-oriented career path.

Personal Value

Personally, this project gives me clarity, confidence, and direction as I pursue a role that better fits my strengths and long-term goals. It also helps me develop habits of intentional career planning and proactive skill development.

Learning Outcomes

Through this project, I will learn how to position myself for career advancement, engage effectively with professionals in my desired field, and tailor application materials to analyst roles. I will also gain insight into real-world expectations for analysts, helping me prepare for future opportunities.

Scope Statement

Scope Statement

In Scope

  • LinkedIn profile optimization
  • Targeted networking and organization research
  • Informational outreach and interviews
  • Documenting transferable skills
  • Tailoring resume and cover letter for analyst roles
  • Submitting initial analyst-focused job applications

Roadmap Items (Beyond Initial Scope)

  • Developing analytics skills (SQL, Excel, Python, Power BI/Tableau)
  • Completing relevant certifications or micro-courses
  • Building an analytics portfolio
  • Expanding applications to additional analyst roles
  • Continuing networking and informational interviews

Out of Scope

  • Applying to a master’s program in Data Analytics
  • Completing long-term certifications (Google Data Analytics, Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst, CompTIA Data+)
  • Developing large-scale business applications
  • Creating a YouTube channel or podcast

Deliverables

Project Deliverables

Deliverable Type SMART Description Definition of Done
Experience & Skills Documentation Tangible Document transferable Help Desk accomplishments and map them to analyst competencies using concise, measurable statements. A completed list of quantified achievements and skill mappings is finalized and incorporated into LinkedIn and resume updates.
LinkedIn Profile Optimization Tangible Update and refine LinkedIn profile to reflect analyst-focused skills, experience, and branding (headline, About, Experience, Skills). LinkedIn profile is fully updated, reviewed by professor/mentor, and aligned with Business/Data Analyst roles.
Targeted Networking & Research Intangible Identify and engage with at least 10 professionals and 5 organizations related to analyst roles through LinkedIn and industry research. Documented list of contacts and organizations; 10+ connection attempts made; engagement activity tracked.
Informational Outreach & Insights Intangible Conduct outreach to 3–5 professionals and complete at least one informational interview to gather role-specific insights. Outreach messages sent, at least one interview completed, and key takeaways documented.
Tailored Resume & Cover Letter Tangible Create a resume and cover letter tailored to analyst-focused roles, aligned with insights from research and interviews. Resume and cover letter drafts completed, reviewed for feedback, and finalized for submission.
Initial Analyst-Focused Job Applications Tangible Submit 2–3 applications for Business/Data Analyst roles or closely related positions identified during the project. Applications submitted, confirmations saved, and entries tracked in an application log.

Methodology / Approach

Methodology / Approach

Agile-Lite, Single Iteration

This project will follow an Agile-Lite methodology using a single, focused iteration — essentially “one iteration because my life is on fire.” Instead of multiple sprints, I will work through one compressed cycle that includes planning, execution, feedback, and refinement. The focus is on delivering usable career assets (LinkedIn profile, networking activities, resume, and applications) within a short time frame while remaining flexible to feedback from my professor and professional contacts.

Methodology Justification

  • Time constraints: With only one week remaining, a traditional multi-sprint approach would create unnecessary overhead. A single Agile-Lite iteration allows me to focus on completing the most valuable work efficiently.
  • Need for flexibility: This project depends on real-world interactions (networking and informational outreach) that may change based on responses and availability. Agile-Lite supports adapting priorities as I receive feedback or new information.
  • Value-focused delivery: The methodology emphasizes producing working increments of value—an updated LinkedIn profile, documented skills, resume, and real job applications—rather than heavy documentation or rigid phase gates. This aligns directly with the goal of transitioning into a Business/Data Analyst role.
